Let's tell you a little about us.

We offer more than Self Catering Apartments, we offer you a home away from home. A place you can lay your head down after a long day, a place you can smile and share stories about your adventures across the land of the brave with your loved ones, a place you can prepare for those daunting business meeting or conferences.
Thus our Staff is well trained and our facilities are clean, organized and well kept, allowing you to worry about nothing but making the most out of your stay.

Highlights
  1. Our apartments offer WIFI to all our guests so that they may never feel disconnected.
  2. Want to feel one with nature, we got you covered. Breath in the fresh clean oxygen from our stunning garden views all while relaxing in your apartment.
  3. If you are feeling a bit adventurous we are centrally located, but a stroll away from the pulse of Windhoek's CBD and many more sites for any kind of day..
  4. If you are feeling a little paranoid about the safety of your vehicle, don't worry and sleep peacefully knowing it is safe in our on site private parking area.
  5. Finally, we offer a fully equipped kitchen, a dinning area to spend with your loved ones, a flat screen TV with satellite channels to catch up on all your favourite shows and of course a private bathroom with a shower.
